Nutrition

  • Eat a balanced diet for energy, growth, and a strong immune system, avoiding processed foods and sugars.

  • Stay hydrated with at least 8 glasses of water daily for better performance and health.

  • Control portions and eat mindfully to manage calorie intake and avoid overeating.

  • Include healthy snacks like yogurt, nuts, and vegetables to maintain energy levels and prevent overindulgence.
